speaking skux language

When being skux, some terms or phrases are either replaced, or added into your language. Of course the typical slang is involved such as "bro" or "nah", but there are some more to master before becoming skux, some examples are below.

Phrases to call Friends.

Man = "Sol'e" (Samoan for Man) Brother = "Uso"/"Uce" (Samoan for Brother) "G" (Samoan for Good Person)

Phrases for Everyday Life

Meal = "Feed" Peace be with you = "Chur Brudda" Goodbye = "Laytah Sol'e" Ask = "Aks" You Guys = "Yous"

Phrases for Food's

Breakfast = "KFC" Morning Tea = "KFC" Lunch = "KFC" Afternoon Tea = "KFC" Dinner = "KFC" Dessert = "KFC" Midnight Snack = "KFC Leftovers"
